Typography and Editorial | Vietnamese Language
and Typography

The aim is to raise awareness of Vietnamese typography by highlighting its unique design challenges and cultural significance. The project will focus on the importance of diacritical marks in readability and legibility, providing a resource for designers working with Vietnamese types. Through a concertina, the goal is to educate, engage, and inspire, ensuring Vietnamese typography is understood and respected in modern design.

The concertina book is an interactive and visually engaging way to learn about the Vietnamese language and its typography. Using typography, the book will also show how type helps to reflect and protect cultural identity.
