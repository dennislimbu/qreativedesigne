Branding | The Vast Collective

The Vast Collective, a culturally and socially engaged gallery, uses a branding process rooted in its core values: inspiration, imagination, and inclusion. Exploring the collective’s mission and the diverse communities it seeks to represent.

 Through research and moodboards, we developed a visual identity that feels open, expressive, and welcoming. This included a bold logo and flexible design elements that reflect creative freedom and cultural dialogue. Every aspect of the brand was crafted to resonate with both artists and audiences, celebrating diversity, encouraging participation,  and sparking imagination.

The outcome gives The Vast Collective a strong and consistent visual presence that reflects its purpose and builds a sense of belonging, ensuring it stands out as a space where creativity and culture come together.
