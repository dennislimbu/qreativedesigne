Product Design | Penguin

A coherent brand identity that speaks to who Penguin are now and what Gen Z want to see. It should exist to enhance the reading experience in some way.

The expressive splashes and soft pastel colours feel more personal and creative, which appeals to a generation that values authenticity and individuality lustrous or corporate looking products. Pastel colours are especially popular with Gen Z because they feel calm, nostalgic and gently, offering a break from the online life

Together, the spatter effect and pastel palette create a design that's eye-catching but soft, making it ideal for social media, whether in bookshelf photos or fashion posts featuring books. For Penguin, this look helps the brand connect with Gen Z by blending classic literature with a modern, relatable style.
